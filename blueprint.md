# LottoGen - AdSense Optimized Lotto Number Generator

## Overview

LottoGen is a professional, multi-page web application designed to provide users with lottery number generation tools, statistical insights, and a comprehensive user experience. It is specifically optimized for Google AdSense approval by providing high-quality, unique textual content and clear navigation.

## Design and Features

*   **Multi-Page Architecture:** Includes dedicated pages for Home, Statistics & Tips, FAQ, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Service.
*   **Modern UI:** Clean, glassmorphism-based design with a fixed navigation bar and a responsive layout.
*   **Dark Mode Support:** Full dark mode support that persists across page transitions using `localStorage`.
*   **Interactive Lotto Machine:** A visually engaging number generator with smooth animations and theme-adaptive falling coin background.
*   **Rich Textual Content:** In-depth articles on lottery statistics, probabilities, and selection strategies to provide unique value.
*   **Transparency & Compliance:** Mandatory Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages to meet AdSense and legal requirements.
*   **Monetization Ready:** Strategically placed Google AdSense ad units (Responsive and In-Article) across multiple pages.
*   **User Engagement:** Integrated Disqus comments on Home and Statistics pages.

## Project Structure

*   `index.html`: Home page with the Lotto Machine and service introduction.
*   `statistics.html`: Rich text content about lottery statistics and tips.
*   `faq.html`: Frequently Asked Questions.
*   `privacy.html`: Privacy Policy (Essential for AdSense).
*   `terms.html`: Terms of Service.
*   `style.css`: Unified styling for all pages.
*   `main.js`: Shared logic for theme, animations, and form handling.
*   `ads.txt`: Authorized Digital Sellers file for AdSense verification.
